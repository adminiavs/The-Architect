## Common Mistakes

### 1. Forcing the Breath
**Error:** Straining to inhale deeper or exhale longer than comfortable.  
**Result:** Tension in diaphragm, increased anxiety.  
**Fix:** Start with 3-5-7 or 3-3-3-3 until capacity builds naturally.

### 2. Upper Chest Breathing
**Error:** Shoulders rise, chest expands but belly doesn't.  
**Result:** Shallow breathing, minimal vagus nerve stimulation.  
**Fix:** Place hand on belly - it should expand on inhale before chest does.

### 3. Irregular Timing
**Error:** Counting too fast or inconsistently.  
**Result:** No rhythm established, system doesn't entrain.  
**Fix:** Use metronome app or counted seconds (one-one-thousand, two-one-thousand...).

### 4. Too Many 4-7-8 Cycles
**Error:** Doing 10+ cycles thinking more = better.  
**Result:** Excessive CO₂ shift, lightheadedness, anxiety increase.  
**Fix:** Maximum 8 cycles per session. Wait 10 minutes before repeating.

### 5. Breathing Through Wrong Openings
**Error:** Box breathing through mouth instead of nose.  
**Result:** Dry throat, reduced nitric oxide production, less parasympathetic activation.  
**Fix:** 
- 4-7-8: In through nose, out through mouth
- Box: Both in and out through nose
